📚 Website History

    
The story of The Cats Arcade

    
    

      

        
🎮 The Beginning (Mid January 2026)

        
In January of 2026, Micah had an idea of an unblocked games website. Fun fact: before the name "The Cats Arcade," the name was actually "tha games website" and it was all plain!

      

      
      

        
🚀 Early Development (Early February 2026)

        
Early February, Micah started to create and make the prototype. It was all buggy but it was still a start.

      

      
      

        
✨ Modern Era (Mid to Late March 2026 - Present)

        
Now with the prototype upgraded and super clean and playable, Micah is adding new games, features, and more! The Cats Arcade has become a fully functional unblocked games arcade with a thriving community.
